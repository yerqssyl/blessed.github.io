ABOUT KAHIRU

     ​Blood. Solely blood... The cycle of the red moon takes 721 years to complete and provides the inhabitants with the greatest spectacle ever known, the Carmine Eclipse.

     Kahiru relates the story of the planet Ruy. Five factions, five elements, five philosophies that will pave the way to multiple disputes where heroes and nonpareils will play a fundamental role.

     Each new era brings prosperity as well as destruction with it. Some factions will flourish, while others will be plunged into darkness.
